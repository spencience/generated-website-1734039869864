Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Digital Archives, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information as we work to preserve digital techniques and innovations.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account information (name, email)
  • Usage data and preferences

How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain and improve our digital preservation services
  • To personalize your experience

  • To ensure the security of our archives

Data Protection

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure backup systems

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Export your data
