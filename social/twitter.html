Twitter: Revolutionizing Digital Communication

Platform Overview

Twitter, launched in 2006, revolutionized digital communication by introducing the concept of microblogging. The platform's 280-character limit (originally 140) created a unique form of concise expression that has shaped modern online discourse.

2006: The Beginning

Jack Dorsey sends the first tweet: "just setting up my twttr"

2009: The Rise

Introduction of retweets and trending topics

2017: Character Evolution

Tweet character limit doubled to 280 characters
